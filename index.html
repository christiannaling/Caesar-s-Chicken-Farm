About Us
"Caesar Chicken Farm"
Welcome to Caesar Chicken Farm, a trusted name in the poultry farming industry with over 3 years of experience. Nestled in the scenic province of Marinduque, our farm is dedicated to raising healthy, high-quality chickens in a sustainable and humane environment.
"Our Product"
At Caesar Chicken Farm, we specialize in offering fresh, farm-raised chickens that are perfect for your table. One of our most popular products is our 45-day chickens, which are raised with care to ensure tender and flavorful meat. These chickens are free from antibiotics and hormones, reflecting our commitment to providing healthy and natural food options.
